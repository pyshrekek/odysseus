Found Land

We landed on Helios' island, but we were STARVING. But we must hold on, I warned my crew. I didn't even want to land here in the first place, and I definitely do not want to anger the gods again.

But that little rascacl, Eurylochus, cheered on his stupid crewmen. I could not convince my crew otherwise, and we ended up slaughtering the cattle. We definitely angered the gods, since the meat was already writhing with bugs within minutes.
